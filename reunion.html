2021 Pennsylvania Hottenstein Reunion

The 84th Pennsylvania Reunion will be held on Sunday, June 13, 2021. The Reunion will begin with an Open House at the Hottenstein Mansion at 11:00AM. The Open House will occur from 11:00Am to 12:30PM.

The Picnic will follow using the facilities at the Kutzown Masonic Lodge, just 5 miles west of the Hottenstein Mansion on Route 222.

Please read the 2021 Newsletter for more details about the Reunion

For more information concerning this reunion, please email Terry at taschure(at)aol.com.

2021 Wisconsin Hottenstein Reunion

The 2021 Mid-West Hottenstein Reunion of the Phillip Hottenstein Line will be held in Hickory Hill Park in Potosi, WI on Sunday, August 15, 2021.
