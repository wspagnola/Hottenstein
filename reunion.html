2022 Wisconsin Hottenstein Reunion

The 88th Mid-West Hottenstein Reunion of the Phillip Hottenstein Line will be held in Hickory Hill Park in Potosi, WI on Sunday, August 21, 2022. The Mid-West Reunion will begin with a Gathering at 11:00am followed by a Potluck and Fellowship at 12:00pm.
